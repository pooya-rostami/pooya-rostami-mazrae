Pooya Rostami Mazrae

Overview​

I am currently a PhD student and researcher at University of Mons (UMONS), Belgium. My research focuses on collaborative software development platforms, studying and solving issues related to the usage of CI/CD tools.

Research Interests​

  • Mining Software Repositories
  • Software Evolution
  • Data Science
  • Data Analysis
  • Data Mining
